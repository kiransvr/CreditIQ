# Go To Market and Client Onboarding

## 1. Positioning

CreditIQ Lite is a practical, explainable credit decision-support product for institutions that want fast implementation with existing internal data.

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile

- Banks and MFIs with manual or semi-manual credit assessment.
- Institutions with branch-driven lending and inconsistent decision patterns.
- Institutions with limited integration readiness in early phase.

## 3. Commercial Packaging (Suggested)

- Package A: Pilot (single institution, limited branches)
- Package B: Growth (multi-branch roll-out)
- Package C: Enterprise (custom integrations and analytics roadmap)

## 4. Onboarding Process

1. Commercial and legal closure.
2. Discovery and data readiness workshop.
3. Data template mapping and dry run.
4. Configuration of score bands and policy thresholds.
5. User role setup and training.
6. Pilot go-live and hypercare.
7. Outcome review and scale plan.

## 5. Business Readiness Checklist

- Signed requirements and scoring policy.
- Identified product and risk owners.
- Approved hosting and security posture.
- Training completed for target user groups.
- Support process and escalation agreed.

## 6. Sales Enablement Assets

- Product one pager
- Demo script and sample dataset
- FAQ for risk and compliance teams
- Implementation plan template
- ROI calculator framework

## 7. Pilot Success Metrics

- Assessment turnaround improvement
- Decision consistency improvement
- Override rate trend and reasons
- User satisfaction score
